Drip Irrigation

Financial assistance up to 55% for small & marginal farmers and 45% for other farmers.
Indicative cost of drip Irrigation system ranges from Rs. 21643 to Rs. 112237 per hectare depending upon the spacing of drip lateral pipes and land size.
Maximum permissible assistance will be restricted to 5 hectare per beneficiary.
